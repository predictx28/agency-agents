# Glassons — 3-Month CMO Marketing Plan (Growth Hacker Mode)
**Q2 2026 | April – June | Markets: Australia & New Zealand**

---

## Executive Summary

Glassons is the dominant affordable women's fashion brand in ANZ, generating NZ$251.5M in Australian revenue alone (FY2025, +15.3% YoY). The brand has strong organic momentum, a healthy 59.3% gross margin, and an active TikTok/influencer ecosystem. This plan focuses on three growth levers:

1. **Amplify AU market penetration** — Australia is the growth engine; we double down.
2. **Convert social reach into owned revenue** — Turn TikTok/Instagram followers into email/app subscribers who buy repeatedly.
3. **Engineer virality** — Build referral and UGC loops that compound customer acquisition.

**Q2 Context (ANZ):** April–June is Autumn/early Winter transition — key buying moments for knitwear, layering, co-ords, and denim. This is also EOFY sale season (June), Glassons' single biggest conversion window.

## Month 1 — April: Foundations & Experiments

**Theme: "New Season, New Obsession" — Autumn drop amplification**

### Growth Experiments to Launch (10 in parallel)

| # | Experiment | Channel | Hypothesis |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Nano-influencer gifting blitz (AU-focused) | TikTok/IG | 50 nano influencers (5K–50K) in AU cities drive higher-trust conversions than 5 macro influencers |
| 2 | "Style your autumn" UGC contest | Instagram/TikTok | Hashtag challenge with prize drives 10x organic reach vs paid equivalent |
| 3 | Post-purchase referral popup (15% off for both) | Website/Email | Referral at purchase intent peak lifts K-factor from ~0.3 to 0.6+ |
| 4 | App download incentive at checkout (10% off next order) | Website | Checkout prompt converts 8%+ of web buyers to app |
| 5 | SMS abandoned cart (3-step sequence) | SMS via Emarsys | SMS recovers 12% of abandons vs email's ~6% |
| 6 | TikTok Shop integration test (AU) | TikTok | In-app purchase removes friction; test 10 SKUs |
| 7 | Pinterest SEO content push (autumn outfits) | Pinterest | Autumn outfit pins drive 5% incremental web traffic |
| 8 | Retargeting with social proof overlay ("X people bought this today") | Meta/TikTok ads | Social proof creative lifts CTR by 20%+ |
| 9 | Welcome email sequence A/B: 3-email vs 5-email | Email (Emarsys) | Extended onboarding sequence lifts first-90-day LTV by 15% |
| 10 | Google Shopping campaign for "autumn outfits NZ/AU" | Google | Capture high-intent search traffic not currently monetised |

### Key Campaigns

**1. Autumn Refresh Drop (Week 1–2)**
- Drop 3 new autumn collections simultaneously across IG, TikTok, email
- 20 micro-influencers receive early access 48hrs before launch ("first look" content)
- Email subject: *"You're first. New Autumn just dropped."* (personalised first name)
- TikTok: #GlassonsAutumn challenge — style a Glassons piece for a chance to win $500 store credit

**2. "Wardrobe Reset" Bundle Push (Week 3–4)**
- Bundle 3 autumn essentials at a 15% saving (email + homepage hero)
- Goal: increase average order value (AOV) from ~NZ$75 → NZ$90
- Segment: lapsed customers (90+ days no purchase) with personalised "you left these behind" email

### Owned Channel Actions

- **Email:** Launch a 5-step welcome flow for new subscribers (currently basic). Steps: Welcome + 15% off → New arrivals → Best sellers → Brand story/sustainability → "Have you bought yet?" urgency trigger.
- **App:** Push notification drip campaign for new app installs. Day 1: Welcome + exclusive app-only deal. Day 3: New arrivals. Day 7: "Your wishlist is selling out."
- **SEO:** Publish 8 autumn outfit guide blog posts targeting high-volume keywords: "autumn outfits Australia 2026", "what to wear in May NZ", "layering outfits women."

## Month 2 — May: Amplification

**Theme: "Mid-Season Drop" — Scale what's working, kill what's not**

By end of April, we'll have data from all 10 experiments. May is about pouring fuel on winners.

### Growth Actions

**1. Scale the top 3 winning experiments from April**
- Expected winners based on benchmarks: Nano-influencer gifting (AU), SMS abandoned cart, Post-purchase referral
- Double budget on winning ad creative sets. Pause bottom 20% of ad sets.

**2. TikTok Creator Partnership Program — "Glassons Insiders"**
- Recruit 10 dedicated AU/NZ creator partners (50K–500K followers) on a 3-month retainer
- Each creator gets: monthly gifting allowance, exclusive early access, performance bonus (% of sales via code)
- Goal: build consistent content pipeline that feels authentic, not ad-like
- Target: 5M combined TikTok views in May from creator content

**3. EOFY Teaser Campaign (Build Anticipation for June)**
- Begin building EOFY waitlist: "Get early access to our biggest sale of the year"
- Email capture popup: "Join the EOFY early access list" (offer: 2-hour head start before public)
- Target: 25,000 EOFY waitlist sign-ups in May

**4. Loyalty / Re-engagement Push**
- Segment: Customers with 2+ purchases in last 12 months but inactive for 60+ days
- Campaign: *"We miss you. Here's what you missed."* — curated new arrivals based on past purchase category
- Offer: Free shipping on next order (no code, auto-applied)
- Expected: 18–22% reactivation rate (industry benchmark: 15%)

**5. Affiliate / Cashback Channel Test**
- Launch on ShopBack (AU) — the dominant cashback platform for Glassons' target demo
- CPA model: pay only on confirmed sales. Low risk, incremental reach.
- Estimated incremental revenue: NZ$40,000–$60,000 in first month

**6. Collaborative Drop — Limited Edition**
- Partner with 1 ANZ-based artist or emerging fashion designer for a 20-piece limited capsule
- Promoted as "Glassons x [Designer]" — creates cultural moment, drives PR coverage, scarcity urgency
- Target press: Vogue Australia, The Iconic editorial, Broadsheet

## Month 3 — June: Conversion & Retention

**Theme: EOFY — Biggest revenue month of Q2**

June is Glassons' most important conversion window. The EOFY sale is the #1 traffic and revenue event. The goal: maximise revenue AND acquire first-time buyers who will become loyal customers.

### EOFY Sale Strategy

**Structure:**
- Week 1 (June 1–7): EOFY Teaser — new markdowns added daily. "Check in every day."
- Week 2 (June 8–14): EOFY Peak — sitewide 30–50% off, email + SMS + paid blitz
- Week 3 (June 15–21): Last Chance + clearance push
- Week 4 (June 22–30): Post-EOFY re-engagement — "You found us during the sale. Now meet us at full price."

**EOFY Execution:**

1. **Waitlist early access (built in May):** 25,000 subscribers get 2-hour head start. Creates VIP feeling and morning traffic spike before media spend kicks in. Subject line: *"You're in. EOFY is live — 2 hours before everyone else."*

2. **Paid media blitz:** Heaviest spend of the quarter. All channels active simultaneously.
   - Meta: retargeting all site visitors from last 60 days, LAL audiences
   - TikTok: Spark Ads from creator EOFY content ("EOFY haul" format performs extremely well organically — amplify the best)
   - Google: Shopping + branded + non-branded ("EOFY sale dresses", "winter fashion sale Australia")

3. **SMS campaign (3-part sequence):**
   - June 8 9am: "EOFY is LIVE. Up to 50% off. Shop now →"
   - June 10 12pm: "Your wishlist items are on sale 👀 →"
   - June 14 8pm: "Last 24 hrs. EOFY ends tonight →"

4. **Creator EOFY hauls:** Brief all 10 creator partners to post "EOFY haul" content June 8–10. Coordinate so they don't all post simultaneously — spread across 3 days for sustained algorithm push.

5. **Flash sale within the sale:** June 12 (midpoint) — 4-hour flash sale: extra 10% off already-discounted items. Email + push notification + SMS. Creates urgency spike mid-sale.

### Post-EOFY Retention (June 22–30)

The best time to convert EOFY buyers to loyal customers is immediately after.

- **New-to-file EOFY buyers** (first-time purchasers): Enter a 3-email post-purchase sequence
  - Day 2: Order confirmation + "here's what else you'll love" (personalised recommendations)
  - Day 7: "Your next look is here" — new arrivals at full price
  - Day 14: "Join the Glassons community" — IG follow + app download prompt

- **Lapsed customers who re-activated during EOFY:** Retarget with "Winter Essentials" editorial — non-sale, full-price content. Remind them Glassons is worth it at full price too.

- **App download push:** EOFY drives highest site traffic of the year. Use pop-up during checkout: "Save an extra 5% when you download the app and complete your purchase there." Target: 8,000 new app installs in June.

### Winter Content Strategy (June ongoing)

- 4 "How to style" editorial blog posts: winter layering, workwear, weekend knits, date night
- TikTok series: "Glassons Winter Lookbook" (10 outfit videos from creators over 2 weeks)
- Email: Weekly "New this week" roundup (every Tuesday, 10am local time — highest open rate window)

## Growth Hacks — The Unconventional Plays

These sit outside the standard channel plan but can deliver outsized ROI:

1. **TikTok Shop Affiliate Program:** Enable any creator — even with 1,000 followers — to earn commission on Glassons products they feature. Zero upfront cost, performance-only. TikTok Shop's algorithm actively promotes shoppable content. Potential to generate 500+ pieces of shoppable UGC with no gifting cost.

2. **"Spot the Glassons" UGC Loop:** Run an always-on Instagram Stories feature where Glassons reposts customers wearing their pieces in the wild. No prize needed — the repost itself is the incentive. Drives ongoing UGC submission and deepens community.

3. **Size-inclusive creator casting:** Intentionally cast 30% of influencer partnerships with plus-size and mid-size creators. Not just ethical — plus-size fashion content dramatically outperforms industry average engagement (1.8x). Differentiates from Zara/H&M who lag here.

4. **Duet/Stitch bait on TikTok:** Post one controversial style opinion video per week ("This is the only way to style a midi skirt"). Designed to generate stitch/duet responses. Each response extends organic reach at zero cost.

5. **Google "dupe" keyword strategy:** Capture high-intent searches like "Zara dupe Australia", "Princess Polly alternative", "affordable version of [trend]". These searchers are actively looking for Glassons-tier product at better value.

6. **Email deliverability weapon:** Segment the bottom 20% of email list (lowest engagement) and sunset them before EOFY. Counter-intuitive but improves open rates across the board, which improves domain reputation, which improves inbox placement for the EOFY campaign — the most valuable send of the year.
